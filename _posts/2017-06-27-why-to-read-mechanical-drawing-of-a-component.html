Today, I got Raspberry PI 3 model B. I just bought it without a case and thought to make it by own. I think to put it between two pieces of plywood using screws.

It has four mounting holes and I bought 3 m.m(estimate) screws to mount it between plywood.

I was going to make holes on plywood by estimate. Just before I found the screws size is over than mounting hole on Raspberry Pi.

Than I searched it's mechanical drawing and got the drawing.

Mechanical Drawing of Raspberry Pi 3 Model B

Now, I have complete dimensions and will make holes in plywood at exact positions in the morning. And will also buy screws for mounting.

So, It's compulsory to read structure of every component before doing some process over it. It can save your time.
